# Problem Statement

## Context
This project is an online pharmacy/order-management website built to simplify medicine ordering, prescription handling, and pharmacy operations. Prior to this solution, customers and pharmacies faced slow, manual processes, frequent errors in order fulfillment and invoicing, and limited traceability for prescriptions and stock.

## Why we built this website
- Reduce friction for customers ordering medicines online (search, prescriptions, checkout).
- Ensure secure, auditable handling of prescriptions (upload, verification, pharmacist approval).
- Automate inventory and order workflows to reduce manual errors and fulfillment time.
- Provide accurate, digital invoicing and reconciliation for accounting and compliance.
- Enable analytics and reporting for pharmacy managers to make inventory and supply decisions.

## Problems before the solution
- Manual phone / walk-in orders produced long wait times and limited SKU visibility.
- Prescription handling was paper-first: risk of loss, illegible instructions, and non-compliance.
- Inventory and orders were tracked across spreadsheets or siloed systems leading to stockouts and overstock.
- Invoices were generated manually or using separate tools, creating reconciliation headaches and billing errors.
- No centralized order status tracking or automated notifications for customers.
- Poor auditing and logging for regulatory compliance (e.g., controlled medicines, prescription history).

## Project objectives
1. Deliver an online portal for customers to browse medicines, upload prescriptions, and place orders.
2. Provide pharmacists an interface to review prescriptions, approve or request clarifications, and manage fulfilment.
3. Automate invoice generation with correct tax, discounts, and insurance/claims handling where applicable.
4. Reduce order-to-delivery times through integrated inventory, courier handoffs, and status notifications.
5. Improve traceability, reporting, and compliance for audits.

## Success metrics (examples)
- Reduce average order processing time by 50%.
- Reduce invoice reconciliation errors to <1% of orders.
- Increase online repeat conversion rate by 30% within 6 months.
- Achieve 99.9% availability for core ordering APIs.

## Constraints and compliance considerations
- Sensitive user and medical data requires strong access control and encryption (data-at-rest and in-transit).
- Payment processing must be PCI-compliant (or use a PCI-compliant third-party gateway).
- Prescription handling must meet local legal/regulatory rules (retention, verification, controlled substances).
- System must integrate with legacy pharmacy systems and courier partners where required.

## Conclusion
The website replaces brittle, manual, and error-prone processes with a secure, auditable, and efficient digital workflow for customers, pharmacists, and administrators — improving speed, accuracy, and compliance.
